Using a browser that blocks third-party trackers is important for privacy

It mostly means that it runs much faster.

Most trackers are invisible scripts that are in the background on a number of websites you visit.

You can not see them, but you can see them slowing down your browser speed.

As of the latest version of Firefox, fingerprints and cryptominers are also blocked.

If you are not familiar with cryptominers, here is an example of how they can affect your browser eexperience.

Maybe you have experienced your computer suddenly becoming hotter and hotter or the battery depleting faster than normal.

That is often the byproduct of cryptominers mining around on your device for information.
